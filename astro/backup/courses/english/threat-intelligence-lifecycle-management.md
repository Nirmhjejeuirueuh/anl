## Overview

Participants will acquire the know-how to support, manage and be equipped with the technical competencies relevant to their roles.

## Learning Objectives

In this training, the participants will

- learn about the phases applicable in the threat intelligent lifecycle including the goals, artifacts, sources, criteria and relevant deliverables applicable in each phase
- learn the key consideration in selecting or using the automation tools - data collection, analysis and visualization; scope and requirements (e.g. SIEM vs intelligence-focused tool)
- understand what an integrated and automated threat intelligence platform would comprise
- through use case review, learn to identify the sources of threat intelligence feeds and to correlate the threats with the related feeds.
