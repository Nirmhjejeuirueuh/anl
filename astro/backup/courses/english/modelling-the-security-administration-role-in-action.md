## Overview

Participants will acquire the know-how to support, manage and be equipped with the technical competencies relevant to their roles.

## Learning Objectives

At the end of the training, the participants will

- learn of the activities and responsibilities of the security administration role
- acquire the knowledge to correlate the key security due diligence to the respective security administration tasks
- be able to highlight the types of possible threats and risks security administrators may be handling and managing in their operations
