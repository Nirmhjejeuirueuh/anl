Sean Low is a seasoned advisor with over two decades of experience helping organisations across ASEAN translate complex, emerging technologies into measurable business outcomes and strategic growth. He has a proven track record of advising C-suite leaders and guiding multinational corporations and government agencies-such as Siemens, BHP, and GovTech-through digital transformation initiatives that drive revenue and create sustainable competitive advantage.

His advisory focus covers a wide spectrum of deep technologies, including Al, Quantum Computing, Quantum Safe Telecommunications, Cybersecurity, Cloud Applications, Robotics, and Digital Twins. Sean actively engages with the future of technology, having worked on Web3 pre-tokenization management as an industry fellow with the Global Fintech Institute and advised on metaverse strategies while at Unity Technologies. He currently facilitates international technology partnerships as the Trade & Business Development Director for the Israel Economic and Trade Mission. His commitment to industry leadership is further demonstrated by his role as an instructor at Singapore Management University, his completion of the Boston Consulting Group RISE 2.0 program, and his certification Governance Professional from Nanyang Technological University.
