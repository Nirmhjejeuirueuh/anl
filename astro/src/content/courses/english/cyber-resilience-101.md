In an era of sophisticated cyber threats and increasing digital dependency, organizations must go beyond traditional cybersecurity approaches. Cyber resilience focuses on the ability to prepare for, respond to, and recover from cyber incidents while maintaining business continuity. This foundational course introduces the principles and practices of cyber resilience, equipping professionals with the knowledge to build robust, adaptable security postures.

## Course Overview

Organizations are well aware of the consequences cybersecurity lapses may lead to. Having the measures to protect, control and prevent cyber risks is a norm for most organisations. However, with the advancement of technologies and the fast-evolving digital ecosystem, cyber incidents have been on the rise. Cyber resilience, thus becomes a must-have practice, beyond just protection and prevention to restore service disruption and minimize brand damage in the era of rising cyber-crime rates.

This course explores what is required to stay cyber resilient and understand the scope of work required to practice it effectively.

## Learning Objectives

Participants attending this training will achieve the following:

- **Cybersecurity vs Cyber Resilience**: Understand the difference between traditional cybersecurity and cyber resilience
- **Resilience Framework**: Learn about the Cyber Resiliency Framework and its components
- **Business Context**: Understand cyber resilience elements in the context of specific business environments
- **Response Integration**: Learn how cyber resilience integrates with incident response and management
- **Performance Metrics**: Understand IT metrics for measuring and improving cyber resilience
