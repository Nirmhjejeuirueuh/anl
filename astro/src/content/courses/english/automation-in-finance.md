This comprehensive course explores how automation is reshaping financial processes and equips finance professionals with the knowledge to leverage these technologies effectively.

## Course Overview

In accounting and finance, services and process automation are rapidly gaining great traction with the advancement of technology and market evolving demands. Automation in the areas of Planning & Forecasting, Balance Sheet Analysis, Reviews, Reconciliation, Month-end Processes, Analysis & Review, and Regulatory & Statutory Reporting are becoming a common phenomenon.

This workshop aims to help you understand and apply the knowledge of technology selection considerations, defining and validating requirements, and the adoption of best practices in finance automation.

## Learning Objectives

- **Technology Understanding**: Gain insights into key disruptive trends and their impacts on finance operations
- **Automation Opportunities**: Identify transformation undertaken by businesses leveraging automation
- **Risk Awareness**: Understand impacts on businesses and individuals who fail to adapt
- **Strategic Action**: Learn actions to stay relevant and competitive in the evolving landscape
