This comprehensive 3-day course provides a solid foundation for technology professionals transitioning into financial services roles. Participants gain essential knowledge of the financial industry landscape, regulatory frameworks, and technology management practices specific to banking and finance.

## Course Overview

This module will equip participants with the relevant knowledge on how the services and products relevant to technology are managed and delivered in the highly regulated Financial industry.

The course provides a comprehensive understanding of how financial institutions are organized, the regulatory structures, and the technology trends shaping the industry.

## Learning Objectives

At the end of the training, the participants will gain a sound understanding of:

- **Organizational Structure**: How banks are organized and the importance of key reporting structures for regulatory compliance
- **Business Segments**: Various business segments, stakeholders, services, and products served
- **Regulatory Frameworks**: Regulatory frameworks relevant for tech-related functions in finance
- **Technology Trends**: Key technology trends and their relevance to the banking industry
- **Operational Processes**: Front-office, back-office, and middle-office processes and their evolution with digital transformation

## Who Should Attend

This course is designed for:

- Technology professionals transitioning to financial services
- IT specialists moving into banking and finance roles
- Cybersecurity professionals entering the financial sector
- Cloud computing experts targeting financial industry positions
- Software developers and architects working with financial clients
- Consultants and vendors serving financial institutions

## Course Curriculum

### Day 1: Financial Industry Landscape

**Module 1: Introduction to Financial Services**
- Overview of the financial services industry
- Key players and market structure
- Economic role and societal impact
- Industry challenges and opportunities

**Module 2: Banking Organizational Structure**
- Commercial banking operations
- Investment banking and capital markets
- Retail and corporate banking segments
- Regulatory reporting structures and compliance requirements

**Module 3: Financial Products and Services**
- Core banking products (deposits, loans, payments)
- Investment products and wealth management
- Insurance and risk management services
- Fintech innovations and digital services

**Module 4: Stakeholder Ecosystem**
- Customers and client segments
- Regulators and supervisory authorities
- Industry associations and standard bodies
- Technology vendors and service providers

### Day 2: Regulatory and Compliance Framework

**Module 5: Financial Regulation Fundamentals**
- Regulatory landscape and key authorities (MAS, FCA, etc.)
- Prudential regulation and capital requirements
- Consumer protection and fair treatment rules
- Anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing

**Module 6: Technology-Specific Regulations**
- Data protection and privacy laws (PDPA, GDPR)
- Cybersecurity and resilience requirements
- Technology risk management frameworks
- Regulatory reporting and disclosure obligations

**Module 7: Compliance Management**
- Compliance program structure and governance
- Risk assessment and control frameworks
- Regulatory change management
- Audit and examination processes

**Module 8: Technology Governance in Finance**
- IT governance frameworks and standards
- Technology strategy and planning
- Vendor management and third-party risk
- Technology budget and resource management

### Day 3: Technology in Financial Operations

**Module 9: Front-Office Technology**
- Trading systems and market data platforms
- Customer relationship management (CRM)
- Digital banking and customer-facing applications
- Omnichannel service delivery

**Module 10: Middle-Office Technology**
- Risk management systems and analytics
- Compliance and surveillance tools
- Financial reporting and regulatory systems
- Data management and business intelligence

**Module 11: Back-Office Technology**
- Core banking and account processing systems
- Payment processing and settlement systems
- Treasury and liquidity management
- Operations and reconciliation platforms

**Module 12: Emerging Technology Trends**
- Digital transformation initiatives
- Cloud adoption and modernization
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning
- Blockchain and distributed ledger technology
- API banking and open finance
