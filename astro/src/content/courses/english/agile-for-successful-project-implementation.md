## Course Overview

Know the difference of being Agile rather than doing Agile and understand the organizational impact and value of Agile on businesses. This interactive course provides a fundamental view of Agile, not as a process change, but a culture change. Through hands-on simulations and real-world case studies, you'll experience Agile principles firsthand and learn how to implement them effectively within your teams, departments, and organizations.

## Learning Objectives

At the end of the course, participants will be able to:

- **Define Agile Principles**: Articulate the core values and principles of Agile methodology and understand why leading organizations are adopting it across industries
- **Identify Business Drivers**: Describe the key drivers compelling companies to embrace Agile transformation and the competitive advantages it provides
- **Master Agile Methodologies**: Explore various Agile frameworks (Scrum, Kanban, SAFe) and gain practical experience through interactive Scrum simulations
- **Develop Implementation Roadmaps**: Understand the organizational prerequisites for Agile adoption and create a customized Agile transformation roadmap for your context
- **Navigate Cultural Change**: Recognize the cultural and mindset shifts required for successful Agile implementation
- **Measure Success**: Learn how to define and track meaningful Agile metrics and KPIs

## Who Should Attend

This course is designed for:

- Business and department heads leading digital transformation initiatives
- Managers in business analysis, development, testing, and maintenance functions
- Project managers transitioning from traditional to Agile methodologies
- Product owners and delivery managers responsible for service delivery
- Team leads preparing to implement Agile practices
- Anyone involved in projects requiring increased flexibility and faster time-to-market

## Course Curriculum

### Day 1: Agile Foundations & Principles

**Module 1: Introduction to Agile**
- The evolution of project management
- Waterfall vs. Agile: Understanding the paradigm shift
- The Agile Manifesto: Values and principles
- Being Agile vs. Doing Agile: The critical difference

**Module 2: Business Drivers & Value**
- Why organizations embrace Agile
- Digital transformation and market demands
- Case studies: Success stories and lessons learned
- Measuring Agile value and ROI

**Module 3: Agile Methodologies Overview**
- Scrum framework deep dive
- Kanban principles and practices
- Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) introduction
- Choosing the right approach for your organization

**Module 4: Hands-On Scrum Simulation**
- Interactive team-based simulation
- Experience Sprint planning, Daily Standups, Sprint Review, and Retrospective
- Understanding roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
- Practical application of Scrum artifacts and ceremonies

### Day 2: Implementation & Organizational Change

**Module 5: Organizational Readiness**
- Assessing organizational culture and readiness
- Identifying barriers to Agile adoption
- Building executive sponsorship and buy-in
- Creating psychological safety for experimentation

**Module 6: Agile Transformation Roadmap**
- Defining your current state and desired future state
- Developing a phased transformation approach
- Quick wins vs. long-term strategic changes
- Managing resistance and driving change

**Module 7: Agile Metrics & Governance**
- Key Agile metrics: Velocity, burndown charts, cycle time
- Balancing predictability with flexibility
- Governance in an Agile context
- Continuous improvement through retrospectives

**Module 8: Practical Application Workshop**
- Develop your own Agile roadmap
- Action planning for your organization
- Addressing your specific challenges
- Next steps and continuous learning resources
