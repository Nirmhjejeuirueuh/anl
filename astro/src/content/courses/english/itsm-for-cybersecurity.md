## Overview

Participants will acquire the know-how to support, manage and be equipped with the technical competencies relevant to their roles.

## Learning Objectives

The participants will learn

- about the range of processes applicable to and relevant to ITSM management including cybersecurity service management
- how to troubleshoot security-related incidents, escalating alerts to relevant stakeholders and analyse root causes and implications of incidents
- how to conduct the problem management lifecycle from diagnosis, prioritisation to the identification and implementation of solutions involving security threats, incidents and vulnerabilities.
