## Overview

Participants will acquire the know-how to support, manage and be equipped with the technical competencies relevant to their roles.

## Learning Objectives

Participants will learn about how the cyber forensics work fits into the cybersecurity incident response and management process; the tasks and activities performed in the various phases of a typical cyber forensic investigation and its expected key activities and key deliverables; the best practices to preserve digital evidence by following standards acceptable for the court examination.
