The financial services industry is undergoing a digital transformation, with cloud computing at its core. This comprehensive 2-day course provides financial sector professionals with essential cloud computing knowledge, focusing on the unique requirements, regulatory considerations, and risk management aspects specific to banking and financial services.

**Secure Today, Agile Tomorrow** - we help financial institutions leverage cloud computing while maintaining regulatory compliance and security. Contact us at enquiry@agilenlite.com to discuss your cloud transformation needs.

## Course Overview

This module will equip participants with the technical competencies applicable for cloud computing roles. The course covers cloud service models, deployment models, cloud computing architecture, and the business considerations for cloud adoption decisions, with specific emphasis on financial sector requirements and compliance.

## Learning Objectives

At the end of the training, the participants will:

- **Cloud Service Models**: Gain understanding of IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS and their applications in financial services
- **Deployment Models**: Understand public, private, hybrid, and multi-cloud deployment strategies
- **Cloud Architecture**: Learn cloud computing architecture and design principles
- **Business Considerations**: Evaluate business drivers and ROI for cloud adoption in regulated environments
- **Financial Sector Case Studies**: Analyze real-world cloud implementations in banking and finance
- **Cloud Economics**: Understand cost optimization and financial modeling for cloud services
- **Security Fundamentals**: Learn cloud security basics and compliance requirements
- **Vendor Selection**: Master criteria for selecting appropriate cloud platforms and services

## Who Should Attend

This course is designed for:

- IT professionals in financial institutions
- Technology managers and architects in banking
- Compliance and risk officers
- Project managers leading cloud initiatives
- Business analysts in financial services
- Technology consultants serving the finance sector

## Course Curriculum

### Day 1: Cloud Computing Foundations

**Module 1: Introduction to Cloud Computing**
- Evolution of cloud computing in enterprise environments
- Key drivers for cloud adoption in financial services
- Regulatory landscape and compliance considerations

**Module 2: Cloud Service Models**
- Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) fundamentals
- Platform as a Service (PaaS) capabilities
- Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions
- Financial sector use cases for each model

**Module 3: Cloud Deployment Models**
- Public cloud characteristics and benefits
- Private cloud implementation and management
- Hybrid cloud strategies and orchestration
- Multi-cloud approaches and vendor management

**Module 4: Cloud Architecture Principles**
- Scalability, elasticity, and resilience
- High availability and disaster recovery
- Security architecture and compliance frameworks
- Performance optimization and monitoring

**Module 5: Business Case Development**
- Cost-benefit analysis for cloud migration
- ROI calculation and financial modeling
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Regulatory compliance and audit considerations

### Day 2: Financial Sector Applications and Implementation

**Module 6: Cloud in Banking and Finance**
- Core banking system modernization
- Payment processing and transaction systems
- Risk management and analytics platforms
- Customer experience and digital banking solutions

**Module 7: Security and Compliance**
- Data protection and privacy regulations
- Financial industry security standards
- Compliance automation and reporting
- Incident response and business continuity

**Module 8: Cloud Migration Strategies**
- Assessment and planning methodologies
- Migration approaches (lift-and-shift, refactor, rebuild)
- Testing and validation procedures
- Change management and stakeholder engagement

**Module 9: Vendor and Platform Selection**
- Cloud provider evaluation criteria
- Service level agreements and performance metrics
- Cost optimization and financial management
- Partnership and ecosystem considerations

**Module 10: Implementation Workshop**
- Case study analysis and solution design
- Cloud architecture diagramming
- Migration planning exercises
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
